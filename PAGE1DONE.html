About

Hi there! My name is Laura Jakstys. I am currently a student at Frederick Community College who is studying Graphic Design. Before my time at FCC, I took a semester at Shepherd University and later transferred. Aside from school, I am an entrepreneur running a small business and I have many hobbies and interests.

Although I do not know exactly what I will do in terms of a career in the future, I have many possible options and paths that interest me. My goal is to learn a little about everything so I will not be a one-trick pony. I enjoy things like design, cooking/baking, health and wellness, creative writing, and event planning.

One thing that I love to do and plan to do more in the future is travel. I have so many bucket-list travel destinations to share. I love being spontaneous and jumping on opportunities that could change my life and teach me so much. If I were offered some kind of crazy opportunity to take a flight to another country for a year, I would probably do it.
