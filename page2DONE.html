My Media Recommendations

One of my favoite movies is Atonement. It is a very sad but also very beautiful movie. The main actors/actresses (Kiera Knightley and James McAvoy) are amazing. I give this movie a 9.75 out of 10!

An amazing book recommendation is Between Shades of Grey by Ruta Sepetys. This book is also very sad as it is about Lithuanian people being deported to Siberia during WW2 by Soviet police. I saw the movie for this book and it is just as heartbreaking. I would give this book a 9.5 out of 10.

A song I really like is "Don't Dream Its Over" by Crowded House. This song is classic-feeling yet very calm and makes you feel happy. I like pop rock music which is the classification of this song.

Drama 

My favorite movie genre!

I enjoy dramatic movies that have a lot going on rather than romantic comedy/comedy style movies. I also enjoy the occassional dystopian style film as well.      

Mystery

My favorite book genre!

I enjoy mystery books because they always keep me wanting to turn the page in my books. I like a mystery that is very interesting and not too scary or hard to follow along with.  

House

My favorite genre of music! 

I love house music as it always makes me want to get up and dance. This type of music is very popular in Eastern Europe. Some of my top artists are Galantis, Swedish House Mafia, Disclosure, and Sonny Fodera.      

Documentary

My favorite type of videos to watch!

I love documentaries as long as they are about something that interests me. One of the most interesting ones I have seen was about people who live in very below-freezing climates deep in Siberia.
