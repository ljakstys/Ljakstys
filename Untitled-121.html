My Favorite Hobbies

Sewing

One of my favorite hobbies is sewing. I first learned to sew in 5th grade at the local sewing/yarn supply store. One of my favorite things to do is tailor my own clothes by hemming them or taking them apart and sewing them in a different way.    

Pilates/Yoga

Another hobby I really enjoy is pilates and yoga. I think these are a great way to get exercise and it is fun at the same time! Pilates and yoga can really help strengthen certain muscles in your body that aren't used very often. Bonus points if it is yoga on the beach!    

                          Swimming

I absolutely love to swim in the summer. Swimming is my favorite summer activity because you can be in the sun and cool off in the water. I especially love swimming in the ocean and in lakes.
